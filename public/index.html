ZoneStar is a new location based anonymous social platform for the iPhone.
Use it for local gossip and inside jokes, observations or questions about an area you're in, or just to see what the people around you are thinking.

ZoneStar works by picking up messages as far as 4km from your present location, allowing you to view all posts ordered chronologically or by their star (upvotes) score.

Location Based

Drop a message on your physical location and pick up messages dropped by others around you.

Score System

Upvote messages you like by giving them a star, and keep score of all the stars you receive.

Social Platform

Messages stay on that geolocation forever, so be creative with what you share.
