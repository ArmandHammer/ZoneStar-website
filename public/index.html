Have you ever been to a new city and wanted to find something fun to do? Have you ever wondered what the lineups and drink specials are like at nearby nightlife spots? ZoneStar is the first real-time party app to help satisfy your social cravings. And the best part? No signup required!

You can use ZoneStar to:

  • Find out about the club and bar lineups near you
  • Explore the hottest, most glamorous parties, events and concerts in your zone
  • Discover where the ladies nights, cheap drinks and popular after parties are at

    • The Basics

    Location Based

    Drop a message on your physical location and pick up messages dropped by others around you.

    Score System

    Upvote messages you like by giving them a star, and keep score of all the stars you receive.

    Social Platform

    Mingle with the people around you who are also looking for fun things to do. New friends are just right around the corner.

    New Post

    Leave a message anonymously or with a signature of your choice.

    Talk about the hottest parties and nightlife spots, ask a question about your area, promote a public event or engage other users.
    Always hosting popular events? Now you can advertise your events to the people around you, and feel like a star in your own zone!








    Zone

    The Zone button shows you the exact location and address of a message. Get an inside scoop on all of the interesting things happening around you in real-time, and start exploring exciting new opportunities in your zone.








    Zone Radius

    ZoneStar gives users the flexibility in deciding how far messages will be coming from. With the "Zone" always relevant to their current location, users can adjust their "Zone Radius" according to how far they're willing to explore.

    Flag/Delete System

    Take a stand against offensive messages.

    Report spam or distasteful posts by double tapping on the post, and help keep the maturity level of this social platform high.
    Similarly, if a post you dropped is no longer relevant and you want to delete it, double tap on it from your "Portfolio" tab.
