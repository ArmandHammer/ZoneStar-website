Reignite Your Social Life

Have you ever been to a new city and wanted to find something fun to do?

Remember what your freshman years were like in university or college?

Facebook, Instagram and Twitter is all about knowing the right people. We are here to challenge the notion that you need to have the right connections to get the most out of your social life.

With ZoneStar, it's no longer about who you know. It's about where you are.

Get it on the iPhone

How It Works

Location Based

Leave messages and photos at your location to chat about bars, club lineups, parties and other hot social events.

Score System

Upvote posts you like and gain fame for your photos and messages in your community.

Social Platform

Chat with your community to share, discover and discuss social events happening in your zone.

New Message

Talk about the hottest parties and nightlife spots, ask a question about your area or engage other users.
Hosting a public event? Use ZoneStar to advertise to the people nearby and bring people to your zone!








Zone

Found an interesting event that people are talking about? Head over there and start making new friends.








Zoning In

Adjust the slider to pick up posts from as close as 100 meters, or as far as the whole planet.

Flag/Delete System

Double tap a message to flag it for spam or offensive content.
Double tap on your own messages from the Portfolio tab to delete them.
