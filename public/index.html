Reignite Your Social Life

Leave messages at your physical location, earn Fame points and feel like a star in your own zone.

ZoneStar is an anonymous location-based social platform to help satisfy your social cravings.

Use it to talk about the hottest parties and events, spread rumours and observations, or share a memory. No signup required!

Get it on the iPhone

How It Works

Location Based

Drop messages on your location and pick up messages around you!

Score System

Upvote messages you like and keep track of your own Fame score!

Social Platform

Mingle with the people in your area about public events happening nearby!

New Message

Talk about the hottest parties and nightlife spots, ask a question about your area or engage other users.
Hosting a public event? Use ZoneStar to advertise to the people nearby and bring the party to your zone!








Zone

The Zone button gives you the location of a message, giving you an inside scoop on all the interesting things happening around you in real-time.








Zoning In

Adjust the slider to pick up posts from as close as 100 meters, or as far as the whole planet.

Flag/Delete System

Double tap a message to flag it for spam or offensive content.
Double tap on your own messages from the Portfolio tab to delete them.
