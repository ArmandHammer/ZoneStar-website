New Message/Reply

Users can leave a message anonymously or with a signature of their choice. The more creative/funny/helpful your posts are, the more stars you'll receive from your community.

Zone

The Zone button on a post shows you the exact location of where that post was dropped. The high quality satellite map gives a realistic representation of what the area looks like.

Zone Radius

Picking up too many messages? Use the slider to decrease your zone radius to as close as 100 meters to receive more personal messages. Equivalently, increase the zone radius to pick up messages as far as 4km from your present location.
