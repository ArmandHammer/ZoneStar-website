New Message/Reply

Leave a message anonymously or with a signature of your choice. Talk about the hottest parties and nightlife spots, ask a question about your area, promote a public event or engage other ZoneStar users by replying to their posts. Be as clever and witty as you want to be; your posts never disappear from that location!

Zone

The Zone button shows you the exact location and address of the post. Gain a better understanding of what a message is about by seeing where it was specifically dropped!

Zone Radius

Picking up irrelevant messages? ZoneStar gives users the flexibility in deciding how far the posts will be coming from. With the "Zone" always relevant to their current location, users can use a slider to decrease the "Zone Radius" to as close as 100 meters and pick up what people are doing around their immediate location!

Flag/Delete Post

Offensive and insulting messages are not something ZoneStar users take lightly. Report spam or distasteful posts by double tapping on the post; only you can contribute to keeping the maturity level of this social platform high. Similarly, if you want to delete a post you dropped, double tap on it from the "Portfolio" tab.
